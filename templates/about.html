Welcome

Welcome to the "About" page of the Help Desk Mail Tool. This tool has been created to streamline and enhance our work at the Cambium Assessment Inc. Help Desk.

Key Features

The Help Desk Mail Tool offers the following key features to help you with your tasks at Cambium Assessment Inc. Help Desk:

  • For agents, you will be able to use any template available to you in order to generate commonly used emails.
  • For Admins, you will be able to Add new Projects, add a signature to your project, create/edit and delete e-mail templates, and create/edit and delete form fields.

Contact and Support
